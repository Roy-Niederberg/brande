* General Store Information & Services
  * Hours & Location: Open Mon–Sat 9am–6pm, Sun 10am–4pm. Located at 42 Greenleaf Lane.
  * Parking: Dedicated lot behind the building with 2 reserved spots for expectant mothers.
  * Contact: Call 555-0199 or email hello@timbertots.store. Feedback and suggestions are welcome via email.
  * In-Store Experience: We have a small "Discovery Zone" play area for kids. We do not host birthday parties due to space constraints.
  * Catalogs: We do not print paper catalogs (to save trees); our full collection is viewable online.
  * Newsletter: Subscribe via the sign-up box in the website footer for updates.

* Online Account & Technical Support
  * Browsers: Site is optimized for the latest versions of Chrome, Safari, Firefox, and Edge.
  * Accounts: Optional guest checkout available. Registered users earn loyalty points.
  * Login Issues: Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset via email.
  * Data Management: Update addresses in "My Account." We do not have a public API for inventory queries.
  * Errors: If the website glitches, please email a screenshot to our support team.

* Payments, Pricing & B2B
  * Methods: Visa, Mastercard, Amex, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay.
  * Security: 256-bit SSL encryption; no credit card data is stored on our servers.
  * Pricing: We do not offer price matching as we prioritize fair trade wages for artisans.
  * Discounts: Enter codes at checkout (one per order). Bulk discount of 10% available for schools/organizations on orders over $500 (email to apply).
  * Sales: Seasonal sales occur twice a year (Summer/Winter).
  * Cancellations: Orders may be auto-cancelled due to inventory errors or fraud detection (you will be notified via email).

* Shipping, Delivery & Store Pickup
  * Carriers: UPS Carbon Neutral Ground and USPS.
  * Cost: Flat $6.95 domestic. Free shipping on orders over $75.
  * International: Yes, we ship to Canada and the UK ($25 flat rate, 10-14 day delivery).
  * Timelines: Domestic delivery takes 3-5 business days. Delays are rare; contact us if tracking doesn't update for 48 hours.
  * Tracking: A tracking link is emailed immediately upon dispatch.
  * PO Boxes: Yes, we ship to PO Boxes via USPS.
  * Modifications: Shipping addresses or items can be changed only within 1 hour of placement by calling the store.
  * Pickup: "Click & Collect" orders are ready in 2 hours. Bring ID and order number.

* Returns, Exchanges & Warranties
  * Policy: 60-day return window for unused items in original packaging.
  * Process: Initiate via the "Returns Portal" link in the footer to get a label or instructions.
  * Cost: Free returns in-store. Mail-in returns incur a $6 label fee deducted from the refund.
  * Refunds: Issued to original payment method (takes 5-10 business days).
  * Exchanges: We do not offer direct exchanges; please return for a refund and place a new order.
  * Exceptions: Personalized items and Gift Cards are non-returnable.
  * Repairs: We do not offer repair services, but we sell spare parts (wheels/pegs) for common items.

* Product Details & Safety
  * Materials: 100% FSC-certified beech or rubberwood; non-toxic water-based paints.
  * Safety: ASTM F963 and EN71 certified. All items are rigorously safety tested.
  * Age Ratings: Clearly listed on every product page (e.g., 6m+, 18m+, 3y+).
  * Inventory: Popular items restocked monthly. Sign up for "Back in Stock" alerts on product pages.
  * Pre-orders: Available only for special Holiday Collections.
  * Demos: Video demos are available on product pages. We do not offer live video calls.
  * Sizing: Detailed dimensions are listed on every page (we do not sell plush toys, so no plush sizing guides needed).

* Gifting & Loyalty
  * Gift Wrap: Recycled kraft paper wrapping available for $4.00/item.
  * Loyalty Program: "TimberTokens" — Free to join. Earn 1 point per $1 spent. 100 points = $10 reward. Single tier only.
  * Reserving: Items can only be reserved by purchasing them for Store Pickup.
