Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

Qabu ("we", "us") provides AI-powered chat agents for small businesses. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Chat messages you send through our website widgets
  • Facebook messages and comments sent to pages using our service
  • Basic usage data (timestamps, page visited)

How We Use Your Data

Your messages are sent to an AI language model to generate responses. We store conversation logs to improve the quality of answers. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Facebook Data

When you interact with a Facebook page that uses Qabu, we receive your messages and public profile name via the Facebook API. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use Facebook data for advertising or profiling.

Data Retention

Conversation logs are retained for up to 12 months, after which they are deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Security

All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Contact

For questions or data deletion requests: privacy@qabu.net
