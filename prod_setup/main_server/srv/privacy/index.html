Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Qabu is an independent service operated by its founders, based in Israel. We provide AI-powered chat agents for small businesses ("the Service"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Collect

  • Chat messages you send through our website widgets
  • Facebook messages and comments sent to pages using our Service
  • Basic usage data (timestamps, page visited)
  • Temporary chat history stored in your browser's session storage (cleared when you close the tab)

How We Use Your Data

Your messages are processed to generate AI responses and to improve answer quality. We store conversation logs for up to 12 months to support service quality and troubleshooting, after which they are deleted. We do not sell or rent your personal data.

Third-Party Processors

To generate AI responses, we transmit your messages to the following AI providers:

  • Google LLC (USA) — Google Gemini AI model. Privacy Policy
  • Groq, Inc. (USA) — open-source model inference. Privacy Policy

These providers process your messages solely to return a response. No other third parties receive your personal data.

International Data Transfers

Because Anthropic is based in the United States, your messages are transferred outside Israel and the European Economic Area when generating AI responses. These transfers are necessary to provide the Service.

Facebook Data

When you interact with a Facebook page that uses Qabu, we receive your messages and public profile name via the Facebook API. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use Facebook data for advertising or profiling.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you. You may also object to or request restriction of processing. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Children

The Service is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to stored data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

For questions or data requests: privacy@qabu.net
