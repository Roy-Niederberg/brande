About CraftKids Toys

Established in 2019 in the heart of Portland, Oregon, we're dedicated to providing high-quality wooden toys for children ages 0-8. All our toys are made from sustainably sourced wood, primarily beech and maple, ensuring durability and safety. Visit us at 247 Maple Street or shop online!
