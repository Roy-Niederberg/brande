Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2024

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how our AI-powered customer service platform ("Service") collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our Facebook integration or use our administrative interface.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Facebook Interactions

When you interact with our business through Facebook (comments or direct messages), we collect:

  • Your Facebook public profile information (name, profile ID)
  • Content of your comments on our Facebook posts
  • Content of direct messages you send to our Facebook Page
  • Timestamps of your interactions
  • Post content you're commenting on

2.2 Administrative Users

For authorized administrators using our management interface, we collect:

  • Google account information (email address, name, profile picture)
  • Session data for authentication purposes
  • Chat history with our AI system

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Respond to your comments and messages on Facebook using AI-generated responses
  • Provide automated customer service and support
  • Improve our response quality and customer service
  • Authenticate and authorize administrative users
  • Maintain conversation context for meaningful interactions

4. AI Processing

Your messages and comments are processed by artificial intelligence (Google Gemini API) to generate automated responses. This processing:

  • Happens in real-time when you interact with us on Facebook
  • Uses your conversation history to provide contextual responses
  • Is subject to Google's AI services terms and privacy policies

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Facebook Graph API: To receive and respond to your comments and messages
  • Google OAuth: For administrator authentication
  • Google Gemini API: For AI-powered response generation

Each service has its own privacy policy:

  • Facebook Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

6. Data Storage and Retention

  • Facebook interaction data is processed in real-time and retained only as needed for conversation context
  • Administrative session data expires after 30 minutes of inactivity
  • We do not permanently store your Facebook messages or comments on our servers
  • Configuration and knowledge base data is retained for service operation

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Facebook (through their API, as required for the service to function)
  • Google services (OAuth for authentication, Gemini for AI processing)

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data processed through Facebook by contacting us
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out by not interacting with our Facebook Page
  • Control your Facebook data through Facebook's privacy settings

9. Security

We implement security measures including:

  • Webhook signature verification for Facebook events
  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all web traffic
  • Secure credential management through Docker secrets
  • Session-based authentication for administrative access
  • Email whitelist for administrative access control

10. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates when the policy was last revised. Continued use of our Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your privacy rights, please contact us through our Facebook Page or email the administrator of your specific service instance.

13. Facebook Platform

This application is built on the Facebook platform and is subject to Facebook's Platform Terms and Policies. By interacting with our Facebook Page, you also agree to Facebook's terms and policies.
